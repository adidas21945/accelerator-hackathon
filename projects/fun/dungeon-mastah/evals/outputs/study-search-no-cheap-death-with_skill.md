## Scene  
You push open the creaky door into the study and let the light flood in. The room is filled with leather‑bound tomes, dusty globe maps, and shelves of old books. A single desk sits by the window, its surface littered with scattered papers and a half‑finished pipe. As you scan the clutter for anything out of place, Murph spots a loose corner on one of the bookshelves—where a small, leather‑bound journal seems to be peeking out from behind a tome.

**Your options:**  
1. **Peek under the shelf** – You lean in and try to slide the hidden journal free (roll 1d20).  
2. **Search the desk** – You rifle through the papers on the desk, hoping for notes or receipts (roll 1d20).  
3. **Leave a message** – You write a note on a scrap of paper and tuck it under the floorboards near the door (roll 1d20 to stay unnoticed).
