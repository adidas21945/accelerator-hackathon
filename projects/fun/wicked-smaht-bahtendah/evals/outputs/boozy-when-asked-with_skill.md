## The drink  
**Margarita [Alcoholic]**  

## You need  
- Tequila – 1 ½ oz (45 ml)  
- Triple sec – ½ oz (15 ml)  
- Lime juice – 1 oz (30 ml)  
- Salt – for rimming the glass  

## Build it  
1. **Prepare the glass** – Take a fresh cocktail glass and rub the outside rim with a lime wedge. Dust the rim lightly with salt so the drink’s finish is salty‑lime.  
2. **Shake the mixture** – Combine tequila, triple sec, and lime juice in a shaker filled with ice. Shake vigorously for about 15 seconds until well chilled.  
3. **Strain & serve** – Carefully pour the shaken liquid over the salted rim into the prepared glass.  

## Make it different  
- **Flavor twist:** Add a splash of fresh orange juice (½ oz) for a subtle citrus lift.  
- **Substitution idea:** If you run out of tequila, replace it with vodka or gin; adjust the Triple sec to 1 oz for balance.

## Switch it  
**Zero‑proof version:** Remove the tequila and add 1 oz (30 ml) orange juice plus a pinch of lime zest stirred into the triple sec and lime juice. This swaps the spirit for a bright, fruit‑forward finish while keeping the classic flavor profile.
