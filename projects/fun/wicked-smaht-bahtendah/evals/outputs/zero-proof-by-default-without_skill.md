Here’s a refreshing **Mint & Lime** drink perfect for a hot day:

### **Mojito (Alcoholic)**
- **Glass:** Highball  
- **Ingredients:**  
  - Light rum (2–3 oz)  
  - Juice of **1 lime**  
  - Sugar (2 tsp)  
  - Mint (2‑4 leaves)  
  - Soda water (to top)  
- **Method:**  
  1. Muddle the mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in the bottom of a highball glass.  
  2. Add a splash of soda water, then fill the glass with cracked ice.  
  3. Pour in the rum and finish with more soda water.  
- **Garnish:** Sprig of fresh mint & a slice of lime (or muddle‑mint garnish).  

### **Virgin Mojito (Non‑alcoholic)**
- **Glass:** Highball  
- **Ingredients:**  
  - Juice of **1 lime**  
  - Sugar (2 tsp)  
  - Mint (8 leaves)  
  - Soda water (to top)  
- **Method:**  
  1. Muddle the mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in the glass.  
  2. Fill with cracked ice, then top with soda water.  
  3. Stir gently and garnish with a sprig of mint and a lime wheel.  

**Why it’s perfect for a hot day:** The combination of fresh mint, citrusy lime, and effervescing soda creates a cooling, aromatic drink that’s light enough to enjoy on the hottest days—whether you prefer the classic alcoholic version or the refreshing non‑alcoholic twist. Enjoy!
