Here’s a quick **Tequila Sunrise** using only what you have on hand:

### Ingredients
- **Orange Juice** – 2 parts (or as much as you like)
- **Grenadine** – 1‑2 parts (just enough to give the drink its pink hue and sweet finish)

### Instructions
1. **Mix in a highball glass**  
   - Fill the glass with ice.  
   - Pour the orange juice over the ice first.

2. **Add tequila**  
   - Slowly pour in the tequila (the “2 measures” of tequila). The amount you use will depend on how strong you like it—start with about 1 oz (≈30 ml) and adjust to taste.

3. **Finish with grenadine**  
   - Add a splash of grenadine, just enough so the liquid settles into a gentle “sunrise” effect at the bottom of the glass. You can add it drop‑by‑drop until you see the pink hue start to settle.

4. **Stir gently** (optional) – Give the drink a quick stir with a bar spoon or muddler to blend any remaining bubbles without losing the layered look.

5. **Garnish** – A twist of orange peel on top adds that classic finishing touch.

### Serving
- Serve immediately while the layers are distinct and enjoy the sweet, fruity sunrise vibe!

Enjoy your homemade Tequila Sunrise! 🍹
