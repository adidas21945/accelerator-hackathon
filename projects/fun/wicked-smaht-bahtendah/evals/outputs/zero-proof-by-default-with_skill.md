## The drink  
**Virgin Mojito [zero-proof]**  

## You need  
- Lime – Juice of **1** (60 ml)  
- Sugar – **2 tsp** (≈10 g)  
- Mint – **8 leaves**, roughly **1 sprig**  
- Soda water – to top  

## Build it  
1. Muddle the mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in the glass.  
2. Fill the glass with **cracked ice**.  
3. Top with soda water, gently stirring once.  
4. Garnish with a fresh mint sprig and a slice of lime.  

## Make it different  
- **Flavor twist:** Add a splash of pineapple juice for a tropical lift.  
- **Substitution tip:** If you don’t have any mint, use **1 tsp basil** or **1 tsp rosemary** – the herbal note will still give that fresh bite.  

## Switch it  
**Virgin Mojito → Mojito (boozy)** – Add **2–3 oz light rum** and stir gently before topping with soda water.
