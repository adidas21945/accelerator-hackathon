**The drink**

**Shirley Temple [Non‑alcoholic]**  
*You get a sweet, fruity fizz that’s perfect for a sunny day.*

**You need**

- Grenadine – ½ oz (15 ml)  
- Ginger ale – Top the glass with this  
- Orange juice – 1 oz (30 ml)  
- Maraschino cherry – 1  

**Build it**

1. Fill a highball glass almost to the brim with ice.  
2. Slowly pour in the grenadine; let it settle at the bottom.  
3. Top off the glass with ginger ale, leaving a little room for foam.  
4. Give a gentle stir once everything is combined.  
5. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and an orange slice.

**Make it different**

- **Flavor twist:** Add a splash of lime juice (½ oz) for a brighter citrus note.  
- **Substitution:** If you run out of ginger ale, use sparkling water or lemon‑lime soda—just keep the fizz light.

**Switch it**

*Non‑alcoholic → Alcoholic:* Swap the orange juice and grenadine for equal parts bourbon (2 oz) and a splash of simple syrup (1 oz), then stir before topping with club‑soda.
