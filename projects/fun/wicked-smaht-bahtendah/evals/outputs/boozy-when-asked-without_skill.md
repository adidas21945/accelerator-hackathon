**Proper Boozy Margarita (Alcoholic)**  

**What you’ll need**

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Tequila  *(silver or reposado)* | **1 ½ oz** (≈ 45 ml) |
| Triple sec / Cointreau* | **½ oz** (≈ 15 ml) |
| Fresh lime juice | **1 oz** (≈ 30 ml) – preferably freshly squeezed from a key‑lime or green‑ish variety for the best flavor |
| Salt (coarse sea salt)** | For rimming only |

\*If you prefer a smoother, less bitter drink, use Cointreau instead of Triple sec.  
\**Coarse sea salt works best; avoid iodized table salt.

---

### Glass & Prep

1. **Glass:** A classic *copper‑rimmed cocktail glass* (or any rocks glass).  
2. **Rim:**  
   - Squeeze a lime wedge around the rim of the glass, then dip it into coarse sea salt. The salt should cling to the edge only—don’t mix it into the drink.

---

### Mixing Steps

1. **Shake**  
   - In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine:  
     - 1 ½ oz tequila  
     - ½ oz triple sec / Cointreau  
     - 1 oz fresh lime juice  
   - Shake vigorously for about 15‑20 seconds to chill and emulsify the oils.

2. **Strain** (no ice) into the prepared glass with the salted rim.

---

### Serving

- **Optional garnish:** A thin slice of fresh lime or a tiny cherry on top, but many purists prefer just the rim for presentation.
- **Sip slowly**: The drink is best enjoyed at room temperature so you can taste all the citrus and tequila notes without being overwhelmed by ice melt.

---

### Quick Tips

- **Tequila quality:** Silver reposado adds subtle vanilla‑nutty tones; gold or “silver” works fine.  
- **Lime freshness:** Use limes that are bright green, firm, and have a good aroma—older limes can be overly tart.  
- **Salt ratio:** A pinch of salt (enough to coat the rim) is all you need; too much will make the drink feel salty.  

Enjoy your proper boozy margarita! 🍹
