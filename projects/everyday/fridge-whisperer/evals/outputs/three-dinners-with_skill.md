## The plan
**Day 1 – Garlic‑broccoli chicken thighs – serves 2, ~20 min**  
*Marinate boneless chicken thighs in garlic, soy sauce and a pinch of pepper; pan‑sear quickly over high heat then finish with broccoli tossed in olive oil.*

**Day 2 – Broccoli & rice stir‑fry – serves 2, ~15 min**  
*Sauté sliced broccoli in a splash of oil until tender, add cooked chicken thighs (from Day 1 leftovers) and serve over steamed white rice.*

**Day 3 – Veggie‑rich pasta night – serves 2, ~25 min**  
*Boil the dried pasta; sauté garlic and broccoli in butter, then toss with a splash of chicken stock for flavor. Serve topped with extra broccoli and grated cheese (optional).*

## Grocery list
- **Produce**
  - Broccoli — 1 head
  - Garlic — 4 cloves
- **Protein**
  - Chicken thighs – boneless, skinless — 2 lb
- **Pasta & Rice**
  - Dried pasta – any shape — 12 oz (≈½ lb)
  - White rice – pre‑cooked or uncooked for steaming — 1 cup (≈½ cup dry) per serving, total 2 cups
- **Oil**
  - Olive oil — ¼ cup
- **Seasonings**
  - Salt — to taste
  - Black pepper — fresh or ground
  - Soy sauce — 1 Tbsp (optional for chicken)
- **Butter** – only needed for the pasta dish
