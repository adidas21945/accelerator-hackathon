## The plan

**Day 1 – Lentil‑Mushroom Stew (serves 4, ~25 min)**  
- Sauté chopped onions and minced garlic in olive oil; add sliced mushrooms and cook until softened. Stir in cooked lentils, vegetable broth, soy sauce, salt & pepper. Simmer 10 min.

**Day 2 – Mushroom‑Lentil Risotto (serves 4, ~30 min)**  
- Sauté chopped onions and garlic in olive oil; add sliced mushrooms and cook until tender. Stir in cooked lentils, Arborio rice, vegetable broth, soy sauce, salt & pepper. Cook 15 min, stirring frequently.

## Prep notes  

1. **Make‑ahead step:** Cook the lentils ahead of time (or buy pre‑cooked) so you only need to finish cooking each dinner in ≤ 30 minutes on a weeknight.  
2. **Substitution:** If fresh mushrooms aren’t available, use dried porcini or more common button mushrooms; they’ll absorb extra flavor from the broth.  
3. **Leftovers idea:** Both dishes pair well with roasted vegetables (e.g., carrots, zucchini) or a simple green salad for an extra meal on the next night.
