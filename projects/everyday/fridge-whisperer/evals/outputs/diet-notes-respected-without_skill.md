## 1. Spaghetti Bolognese with Garlic Bread  
*(Tonight – quick, hearty, non‑vegetarian)*  

### Ingredients (per serving)
- ½ lb ground beef  
- ¼ cup olive oil  
- 1 small onion, finely chopped  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes or canned diced tomatoes  
- 1 tsp dried basil  
- 1 tsp dried oregano  
- Salt & pepper to taste  
- 1 lb dried pasta (e.g., spaghetti)  
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional garnish)  

### Quick Steps
1. **Sauté** onion & garlic in olive oil until translucent – ~2 min.  
2. Add ground beef; break it up and cook through, stirring occasionally – ~5 min.  
3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, herbs, salt & pepper; simmer 10‑12 min (until sauce thickens).  
4. Cook pasta al dente per package directions; drain.  
5. Toss cooked pasta with the sauce, add parsley, and serve topped with garlic bread (see below).

### Garlic Bread
- Melt 2 Tbsp butter in a skillet over medium heat.  
- Add sliced baguette or crusty roll until golden – ~3‑4 min.  
- Sprinkle with sea salt; optional: spread thin layer of softened butter + garlic.

## 2. Chicken‑Style Stir Fry with Rice  
*(Tomorrow – vegetarian‑friendly, uses the same pantry staples)*  

### Ingredients (per serving)
- ½ lb ground beef (or you can replace with extra chicken strips for a lighter version)  
- 1 Tbsp olive oil  
- ¼ cup soy sauce (low‑sodium if available)  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 small onion, sliced  
- ½ red bell pepper, diced  
- ½ carrot, julienned  
- Cooked white rice (for serving)  

### Quick Steps
1. **Sauté** onions & peppers in oil until softened – ~3 min.  
2. Add garlic and stir for 30 sec.  
3. Toss in soy sauce; cook briefly to deglaze pan.  
4. Stir‑in ground beef, breaking it up as it cooks – ~5 min.  
5. Plate rice alongside the meat mixture.

## 3. Vegetarian Lentil‑Rice Bowl *(Optional Swap)*  

### Ingredients (per serving)
- ½ cup dried lentils  
- 1 cup white rice (or leftover pasta)  
- 2 cups water or chicken stock  
- ¼ cup olive oil  
- 1 small onion, diced  
- 3 garlic cloves, minced  
- 1 tsp cumin seeds  
- Salt & pepper to taste  

### Quick Steps
1. Rinse lentils; combine with water (or broth) and bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer ~20 min until tender.  
2. While lentils cook, sauté onion in oil until soft – add garlic for the last 30 sec.  
3. Stir in cumin seeds, salt & pepper; mix with cooked rice or pasta.
