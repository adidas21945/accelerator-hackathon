## The plan
**Day 1 – Spaghetti Bolognese** (serves 2, ~25 min) – A classic meat‑and‑pasta sauce made with ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomato paste, served over cooked pasta.  
**Day 2 – Veggie Pasta Primavera** (serves 2, ~20 min) – Sautéed bell peppers, zucchini, carrots, peas, and onion in olive oil; tossed with fresh basil and a splash of soy sauce for flavor, served over cooked pasta.  
**Day 3 – Chicken‑Style Stroganoff (Vegetarian Option)** (serves 2, ~25 min) – Mushrooms, onions, garlic, and seasonings sautéed in butter; finished with a quick pan‑cooked egg wash for a creamy coating, served over cooked pasta.
