2. **Prepare Your Home**
   - Secure loose items (outdoor furniture, grills, trash cans) and bring them inside.
   - Reinforce garage doors if they’re not already wind‑rated.
   - Check that your roof is properly attached; consider a quick visual check for any missing shingles or damaged flashing.

3. **Plan Your Travel**
   - Avoid driving through flooded roadways. Even shallow water can cause loss of control.
   - If you must travel, keep headlights on and drive at reduced speed.
   - Keep an emergency kit in the car (flashlight, spare batteries, blankets, bottled water).

4. **Evacuation Zones** – Suffolk County does not have formal mandatory evacuation orders for this event, but high‑risk coastal areas (e.g., Great Kills, Montauk) may be designated by local officials if conditions worsen.
