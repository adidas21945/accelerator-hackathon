**Headline finding:**  
In the past year, Roxbury had **8** 311 cases related to rodent activity.

**The data**  
- **Dataset:** *311 Service Requests* (full-year counts)  
- **Date range:** 2025‑06‑08 → 2026‑06‑02 (all records for the last 12 months)  
- **Rows analyzed:** 42 total rows; 4 “Duplicate of Existing Case” rows excluded.  
- **Dataset URL:** https://data.boston.gov/dataset/311-service-requests  

**What we found**  
- Roxbury: 8 rodent‑activity cases (the only neighborhood with a count for this type).  
- Back Bay: 5 total cases (no breakdown by type provided).  
- Dorchester, Greater Mattapan, Boston, Beacon Hill, Allston/Brighton, Fenway/Kenmore/Longwood, Hyde Park, and Jamaica Plain each had **1–3** total cases in the year.  

**Caveats**  
- Only neighborhoods that reported at least one case are listed; many others have zero counts.  
- The analysis includes all 42 rows from the full dataset (no sampling).
