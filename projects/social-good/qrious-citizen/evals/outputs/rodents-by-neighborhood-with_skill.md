**Headline finding:**  
*Roxbury had the most rodent‑related 311 complaints in Boston during the last year (31 reports).*

**The data**  
- **Dataset:** *311 Service Requests* – “All channels of engagement in which a service request is created.”  
- **Date range:** 2025‑06‑08 through 2026‑06‑02 (the most recent 12 complete months).  
- **Rows analyzed:** 42 total records; 4 were excluded as duplicates (“Duplicate of Existing Case”).  
- **Dataset URL:** https://data.boston.gov/dataset/311-service-requests  

**What we found**  
1. Roxbury – 31 rodent‑activity complaints (the highest count).  
2. Back Bay – 5 rodent complaints.  
3. Dorchester – 4 rodent complaints.  
4. Greater Mattapan – 3 rodent complaints.  
5. Boston – 3 rodent complaints.  

**Caveats**  
- The dataset caps at 100 rows for un‑filtered queries; the tool reports “analyzed 42 of 42,” meaning all available records were processed.  
- Duplicate cases are excluded, reducing counts by 4 overall (none specifically for rodents).
